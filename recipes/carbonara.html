Carbonara

A quick classic Carbonara

This carbonara takes 20 minutes to prepare and is sure to impress the family, guests, or just yourself.

Ingredients
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Spaghetti noodles, or linguine
  • lots of cheese
Steps
  1. Mix eggs with cheese and pepper
  2. Fry bacon, crumble when crispy
  3. Mix egg and cheese into hot noodles.
  4. Add bacon and enjoy.
