Mushroom Chicken Thighs

Savoury mushroom chicken

Only a few ingredients, with a lot of flavour

Ingredients
  • Cream of mushroom soup
  • Chicken thighs
  • garlic
  • rosemary
Steps
  1. Place chicken thighs in baking dish
  2. Mix soup with water and pour over chicken
  3. add garlic and rosemary
  4. bake until chicken is golden
