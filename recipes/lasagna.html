Lasagna

This is a delicious homemade lasagna

This lasagna takes a long time to make but it will impress the whole family. Cook this on a rainy day, or when there is a party to throw.

Ingredients
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Lasagna sheets
  • garlic
  • lots of cheese
Steps
  1. Layer pasta with sauce and cheese
  2. bake in oven for 4 hours
  3. let cool and enjoy
